Treemap Visualisation

This Treemap Visualization explores data in 4 dimensional within 3 levels:
1. In the first level, it shows the department name and the total lifecycle cost of the department.
2. In the second level, it shows Investment title that belongs to the selected department, and the lifecycle cost of the investment.
3. In the third level, it shows project name and project description of the selected investment.
It interacts with users in the following 3 ways:
1. When users hover on the block, the pop up window will show the number of lifecycle cost and the percentage of the block.
2. User could click the block to go to the next level.
3. User could click the level title to back to the previous level.

The visualization is designed for people who want to know the details of the project and its cost. It also helps potential audience know the department structure in a concise way.
